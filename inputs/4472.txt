Experimedia is a design team that supports research and teaching projects in the Division of Literatures, Cultures, and Languages at Stanford University that produces posters, video, and websites in its lab. Originally established by Zach Chandler, the group was reinstated when Mike Widner become the ATS for the DLCL.
