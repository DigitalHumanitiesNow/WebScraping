Bibliopedia is a tool for the discovery and analysis of humanities research, and a platform where scholarly communities can form to discuss, revise, and extend our knowledge of existing research. Initially developed by Michael Widner (now ATS for the DLCL) and Jason Yandell, Bibliopedia is now being used by faculty research projects at Stanford.
