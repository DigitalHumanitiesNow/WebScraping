Call for papers for the 2015 edition of The Philosophy of Computer Games International Conference  The conference will be held 14-17 October in Berlin.   The theme of the conference is “Meaning and Computer Games”.   There is is also an option to submit papers for an “open” category.   The deadline for abstracts is 1 July.  http://gamephilosophy.org/2015/04/call-for-papers-pcg-conference-2015
