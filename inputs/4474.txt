In the 2013 Fall Quarter, Dan Edelstein, Professor of French and, by courtesy, of History, and Michael Widner convened The Digital Humanities Focal Group (DHFG) for the Division of Literatures, Cultures, and Languages. The DHFG promotes faculty and graduate research in the digital humanities through lectures series, praxis workshops, curriculum, and the identification and development of digital humanities research projects, especially those eligible for grant-funding opportunities.
