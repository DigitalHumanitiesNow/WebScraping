The fall 2005 Washington DC Area Forum on Technology and the Humanities is entitled "Massive Digitization Programs and Their Long-Term Implications: Google Print, the Open Content Alliance, and Related Developments." The panelists are Clifford Lynch, Executive Director of the Coalition for Networked Information, and Jonathan Band, a Washington DC attorney and intellectual property advocate. The forum will be held on Monday, November 28 from 4:00-6:00PM at the Arlington Campus of George Mason University.
