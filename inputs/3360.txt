Another discussion on what constitutes the digital humanities was held at Columbia University on April 6, 2011. Questions posed were:  What do digital humanities scholars see as the potential of this  interdisciplinary field? What are the important theoretical and  methodological contributions digital humanities can offer to both the  humanities and the sciences? Panelists included:Daniel J. Cohen, Assoc. Professor  of History and Director of the Center for History and New Media (CHNM)  at George Mason University Federica Frabetti, Senior Lecturer in the  Communication, Media, and Culture Program at Oxford Brookes UniversityDino Buzzetti from the Department of Philosophy at the  University of Bologna
