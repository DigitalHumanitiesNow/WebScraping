Prof. Terence Ranger (Emeritus, University of Oxford)  discusses his many contributions to African Studies and African  History, how these themes have developed, and also his 17th book,  Bulawayo Burning (2010). This is the first of three podcasts recorded at the ‘Making History: Terence Ranger and African Studies’ conference, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign October, 2010.
